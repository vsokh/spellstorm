## Game Description

Spellstorm is a co-op roguelike wave survival dungeon crawler that runs entirely in your browser. Pick a wizard class, team up with a friend, and fight through 20 waves of escalating enemies culminating in a boss finale. No downloads, no installs -- just open the link and play.

Choose from 14 distinct wizard classes, each with 4 unique spells and a passive ability. Whether you want to rain fire as a Pyromancer, freeze entire rooms as a Cryomancer, build turret fortifications as an Engineer, or heal your partner as a Paladin, every class plays differently and rewards mastery.

Spellstorm is designed for two-player online co-op using peer-to-peer connections. Share a room code with a friend and you are instantly connected -- no account required, no central server. The game plays just as well solo if you prefer to go it alone.

Between waves, spend gold at the shop to heal up, boost your stats, or gain temporary power. After each wave, choose from randomized upgrades that stack, evolve, and transform your build. With 120+ upgrades including evolutions and powerful cursed upgrades that trade drawbacks for raw power, no two runs play the same.

Pair complementary classes to unlock one of 10 synergies -- Elemental Clash, Firestorm, Blood Pact, and more -- each granting unique bonuses that reward strategic team composition. Coordination matters.

Procedurally generated dungeons, real-time 60 FPS combat, and roguelike progression make every run a fresh challenge. How far can you push before the storm overwhelms you?

## Features

- **14 Wizard Classes** -- Pyromancer, Cryomancer, Stormcaller, Arcanist, Necromancer, Chronomancer, Knight, Berserker, Paladin, Ranger, Druid, Warlock, Monk, and Engineer. Each class has 4 spells (primary, secondary, ability, ultimate) and a unique passive.

- **2-Player Online Co-op** -- Peer-to-peer multiplayer via room codes. No server, no account, no downloads. Share a code and play instantly.

- **Solo-Friendly** -- Every run is fully playable alone. Scale is adjusted for single-player.

- **120+ Upgrades** -- Stackable upgrades with diminishing returns, evolution upgrades that transform your spells, and cursed upgrades that offer big power at a cost.

- **10 Class Synergies** -- Pair specific classes to unlock powerful team bonuses:
  - Elemental Clash (Pyromancer + Cryomancer)
  - Firestorm (Pyromancer + Stormcaller)
  - Frozen Lightning (Cryomancer + Stormcaller)
  - Holy Bulwark (Knight + Paladin)
  - Blood Pact (Berserker + Necromancer)
  - Time Warp (Arcanist + Chronomancer)
  - Arsenal (Ranger + Engineer)
  - Dark Nature (Druid + Warlock)
  - Inner Light (Monk + Paladin)
  - Dark Covenant (Necromancer + Warlock)

- **Shop System** -- Spend gold between waves on Health Potions, Vitality Charms, Power Shards, Ward Stones, and Swift Boots to adapt your strategy on the fly.

- **20 Waves + Boss Finale** -- Enemy waves escalate in difficulty with new enemy types, increased spawns, and tougher compositions. Survive all 20 to face the final boss.

- **Procedural Dungeons** -- Randomly generated room layouts with pillars, corridors, and combat arenas. Every run has a different map.

- **Plays in Your Browser** -- HTML5 Canvas game. No download, no plugin. Works in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and other modern browsers.

- **Real-Time Combat at 60 FPS** -- Smooth, responsive action with projectiles, beams, zones, homing spells, leaps, traps, and area-of-effect abilities.

## Controls

### Keyboard

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W / A / S / D | Move |
| Left Click | Primary spell |
| Right Click | Secondary spell |
| Q | Ability |
| R | Ultimate (when charged) |
| Shift | Dash (unlocked via upgrade) |
| B | Open Shop (between waves) |
| ESC | Close menus |
| F11 | Toggle fullscreen |
| F3 | Performance overlay |

### Mouse

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Mouse position | Aim direction |
| Left Click | Cast primary spell |
| Right Click | Cast secondary spell |
